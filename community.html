Why Partner with Us

The Region Succeeds
When We Build Together

Schuylkill County's economic future depends on a pipeline of skilled, curious, STEM-ready students. That pipeline is built in classrooms, makerspaces, and competitions — and it's built better with you.

Partnering with STEM @ Skook29 gives your organization direct access to the region's future workforce — while making a real, visible difference in education for the kids in your community.
