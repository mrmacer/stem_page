Why STEM @ Skook29

More Than a Classroom.
A Launch Pad.

STEM @ Skook29 exists to open doors. Whether you want to build robots, code apps, lead your school, or explore what a STEM career could look like — we connect you to experiences that make it happen.

Every program we offer is designed with one goal: helping you grow, compete, create, and connect — with real skills for a real future.
